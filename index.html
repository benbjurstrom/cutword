Frequently Asked Questions

Can I preview my edits before exporting?

Yes! CutWord includes an integrated video player that lets you preview your entire edit in real-time. You can see exactly how your segments flow together and make adjustments on the fly.

Does CutWord edit my original video files?

No. CutWord creates metadata-only timelines, preserving your original footage intact.

What export options are available?

CutWord offers flexible export options. You can export a FCPXML file for Final Cut Pro or DaVinci Resolve, export the entire edited video as a single file, or export each segment individually for further refinement.

What framerates and resolutions does CutWord support?

CutWord supports all common framerates and resolutions. The exported FCPXML timeline will match the original frame rate and resolution of the imported video.

How precise is the editing control?

Very precise. You can navigate to any segment by clicking its timecode, manually include or exclude segments, and refine IN/OUT points frame-by-frame to eliminate awkward cuts.

Do I need ongoing internet connectivity to use CutWord?

No. Once the Speech to Text model has been downloaded, CutWord runs entirely offline, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I customize the command words?

Yes, you can edit your IN/OUT phrases in the settings menu.

What Mac hardware do I need?

CutWord requires a Mac with an Apple M1 chip or newer.
