Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use CutWord with any video editor?

Currently supports Final Cut Pro and DaVinci Resolve. More coming soon.

Is my media altered?

No. CutWord only exports metadata. Your original video is untouched.

Can I customize command words?

Yes, you can edit your IN/OUT phrases in the settings menu.

Does it support variable frame rate recordings?

Yes. We account for frame-rate and round where needed for NLE compatibility.
